Start point is outside, endpoint is definitely inside.

Note: Even though one might think using >= etc operators would produce the same result, that is not the case. We rely on the fact that nothing is clipped unless the point is truly outside the rectangle! Without this handling lines ending on the edges of the rectangle would be very difficult.
